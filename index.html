About the page

Learn Sciences With Aliyu Abdu Nasir is an educational platform dedicated to simplifying complex science topics. Whether you're a university student or in secondary school, you'll find valuable resources to improve your understanding of subjects like Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Engineering.We believe that education should be accessible to everyone, and learning in your native language can make it even easier. That’s why we focus on explaining science in Hausa, ensuring that every student can learn in a way that makes sense to them.

Why I Started This Platform

Many students struggle with science because it is often taught in a way that is too technical or difficult to understand. I created this platform to bridge that gap—especially for Hausa-speaking learners. My goal is to ensure that every student has the chance to learn science in a way that feels natural and easy.

What Next

In the future, I plan to expand the subjects I cover, adding even more valuable content. But for now, I am focused on delivering the best educational materials for university and secondary school students.
